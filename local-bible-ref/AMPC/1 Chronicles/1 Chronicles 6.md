Their dwelling places are according to their settlements within their borders: to the sons of Aaron of the families of the Kohathites, for theirs was the [first] lot-- 

<sup>55</sup>

<sup>56</sup>

<sup>58</sup>

<sup>59</sup>

<sup>60</sup>

<sup>61</sup>

<sup>62</sup>

<sup>63</sup>
